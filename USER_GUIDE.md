
# Gym Tracker – User Guide

Welcome to your personalized **Gym Tracker**! This app is designed to seamlessly integrate into your workout routine, providing intelligent tracking, granular muscle visualisations, and effortless syncing across all your devices.

## What is this app?
Gym Tracker is an installable web app (PWA) built specifically around a Push / Pull / Legs / Core split. It completely replaces your notes app or spreadsheet, automatically calculating your progress, tracking your personal records, and visualising your weekly training volume directly on an anatomical body diagram.

---

## How to Install (Add to Home Screen)
Because Gym Tracker is a Progressive Web App (PWA), you can install it directly to your phone without using an App Store.

**On iOS (Safari):**
1. Open the [app link](https://gym-tracker-jc4.pages.dev/) in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. The app will now launch full-screen from your home screen like a native app!

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the [app link](https://gym-tracker-jc4.pages.dev/) in Chrome.
2. Tap the **Three Dots** menu icon in the top right.
3. Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.

---

## Getting Started
1. Open the app and log in with your Google Account.
2. On the **Log** tab, pick your day (e.g., "Push · Chest Day" or "Push · Shoulders Day").
3. Add exercises and log your sets.
4. When you're done with your workout, tap **Log Day** to save the entire session to your history.
5. Check the **Heatmap** tab to see your progress light up!

## Key Features

### 🏋️‍♂️ Intelligent Session Builder
- **Drafts Save Automatically**: Select your day type (Push · Chest, Push · Shoulders, Pull, Legs, or Core) and start logging. If you accidentally close the app or switch tabs, your draft is safely stored on your device.
- **Visual Muscle Picker**: Not sure what to hit? The app displays an interactive body diagram. Tap a specific muscle group (e.g., Lats, Triceps, or Lower Abs) to highlight the exact anatomical fragment on the model, then press **SELECT** to see all targeted exercises for that area.

### ☁️ Work Offline, Sync Anywhere
- **Offline First**: Gyms often have terrible reception. The app writes everything locally first. Log your entire workout without a single bar of cell service; the app will silently sync your data to the cloud the second you regain connection.
- **Multi-Device Support**: Sign in with your Google account and your data lives in the cloud. Log your sets at the gym on your phone, then review your weekly heatmap at home on your laptop.

### 📝 History Management
- Made a mistake? Navigate to the **History** tab to see a reverse-chronological feed of every workout you've ever done. Expand any session to edit weights, reps, or completely delete accidental logs.
